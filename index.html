§ A Abstract

This journal documents the development and analysis of systematic trading strategies through rigorous backtesting methodologies. Each entry examines a specific research question, outlines the empirical framework, and reports results with attention to statistical validity, overfitting risk, and out-of-sample robustness. Equal parts notebook and archive — ideas in progress, strategies under construction.
